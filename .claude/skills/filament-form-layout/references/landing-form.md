# Landing Or Complex Content Form

Apply this layout to slider, landing page, campaign, pricing, and marketing block forms.

## Rules

- Use `Grid` plus sidebar.
- Keep image, title, content, pricing, CTA, and feature blocks in the main content area.
- Keep order, animation, and visibility controls in the sidebar.
- Prefer collapsible sections for optional or rarely edited settings.
- If the form fits on one screen, prefer sections instead of tabs.
